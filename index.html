About Me

Functionize Ltd was founded in 2012 by Julian Higman, after more than 20 years of development experience in a range of sectors including finance, telecoms, defence and online marketing.

With previous experience in C, Ada, Java and PHP, the work undertaken by Functionize Ltd has focused on Ruby on Rails.

Intermittent blogging at https://julianhigman.com
